About Pizza Palace

Our story, our passion, and our commitment to delivering the best pizza experience

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Pizza Palace began as a small family-owned restaurant with a simple mission: to create the most delicious, high-quality pizza using the freshest ingredients.

What started as a neighborhood pizza shop has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our authentic recipes, generous portions, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

We still use the same secret family dough recipe that our founder's grandmother brought from Italy, and we still hand-toss every pizza to ensure the perfect crust.

Our Ingredients

At Pizza Palace, we believe that great pizza starts with great ingredients. That's why we source only the finest:

  • Fresh, never frozen mozzarella cheese
  • Vine-ripened tomatoes for our signature sauce
  • Hand-selected vegetables delivered daily
  • Premium meats from local, trusted suppliers
  • Organic flour for our hand-tossed dough

We never use preservatives, artificial flavors, or shortcuts. Every pizza is made to order with care and attention to detail.
